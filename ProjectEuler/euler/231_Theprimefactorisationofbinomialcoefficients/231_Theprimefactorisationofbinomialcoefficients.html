The prime factorisation of binomial coefficients

Problem 231

Published on Friday, 6th February 2009, 01:00 pm; Solved by 3775; Difficulty rating: 40%

The binomial coefficient 10 C 3 = 120.
120 = 2 3 × 3 × 5 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 3 × 5, and 2 + 2 + 2 + 3 + 5 = 14.
So the sum of the terms in the prime factorisation of 10 C 3 is 14.

Find the sum of the terms in the prime factorisation of 20000000 C 15000000 .
