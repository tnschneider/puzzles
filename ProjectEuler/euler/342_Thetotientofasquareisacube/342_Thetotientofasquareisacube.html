The totient of a square is a cube

Problem 342

Published on Saturday, 11th June 2011, 01:00 pm; Solved by 502; Difficulty rating: 50%

Consider the number 50.
50 2 = 2500 = 2 2 × 5 4 , so φ(2500) = 2 × 4 × 5 3 = 8 × 5 3 = 2 3 × 5 3 . 1
So 2500 is a square and φ(2500) is a cube.

Find the sum of all numbers n, 1 &lt n < 10 10 such that φ(n 2 ) is a cube.

1 φ denotes Euler's totient function .
