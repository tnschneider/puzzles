Let R( M , N ) be the number of lattice points ( x , y ) which satisfy M < x ≤ N , M < y ≤ N and is odd.
We can verify that R(0, 100) = 3019 and R(100, 10000) = 29750422.
Find R(2·10 6 , 10 9 ).

Note : represents the floor function.
